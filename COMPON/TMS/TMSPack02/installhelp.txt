* Installing the TMS Component pack help in Delphi or C++Builder *
******************************************************************

Installing online help
----------------------

-----------------------------
Delphi 5,6,7 & C++Builder 5,6
-----------------------------

For Delphi 6 & 7, first add the file TMSGridPackDx.als to the {$DELPHI}\Help dir.

In the Delphi or C++Buider Help menu, choose Customize
and add TMSPackXX.hlp to the Index tab.

-----------
Delphi 2005
-----------

Execute : RegHelp2 /i TMSPackdBDS3W.hxx

-----------------------------
Delphi 2006 & C++Builder 2006
-----------------------------

Execute : RegHelp2 /i TMSPackdBDS4W.hxx


-----------
Delphi 2007
-----------

Execute : RegHelp2 /i TMSPackdBDS5W.hxx
